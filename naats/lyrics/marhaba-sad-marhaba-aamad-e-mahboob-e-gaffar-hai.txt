Marhaba Sad Marhaba Ya Rasool-Allah Ya Habib Allah ,
Ay Imamul-Ambiya Zeenat-e-Arsh-e-Mu’alla ,

Marhaba Sad Marhaba Aamad-e-Dildaar Hai.

Marhaba Sad Marhaba Aamad-e-Salaar Hai.

Marhaba Sad Marhaba Aamad-e-Mukhtaar Hai.

Marhaba Sad Marhaba Aamad-e-Raajdaar Hai.
08) Unsa Jaisa Chehra Khuda Ne Baya Hi Nahi
Marhaba Sad Marhaba Aamad-e-Rukhsaar Hai.

Marhaba Sad Marhaba Aamad-e-Qasim-o-Jabbar Hai.

Marhaba Sad Marhaba Aamad-e-Taajdaar Hai.
12) Qibla Badal Jati Hai Jab Hoti Hai Unki Marzi
Marhaba Sad Marhaba Aamad-e-Waqaar Hai.
13) Shafe’a E Mehshar Me,Bas Ek Wahi Hai Yaavar
Marhaba Sad Marhaba Aamad-e-Madadgar Hai.
14) Hashmi-o-Quraishi,Makki-o-Madani Hai Mera Nabi
Marhaba Sad Marhaba Aamad-e-Ghumkhuwar Hai.
15) Imamul-Ambiya Hai Wo Dono Aalam K Dulha
Marhaba Sad Marhaba Aamad-e-Sardaar Hai.
16) Aap Muzammil-o-Mubassir-o-Mudhakkir Hai(N)
Marhaba Sad Marhaba Aamad-e-Naamdar Hai.

Marhaba Sad Marhaba Aamad-e-Sarkaar Hai.
